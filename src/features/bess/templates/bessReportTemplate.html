1.4 Project Electrical Scheme

The project has a capacity of MW / MWh. The DC system will operate at a maximum of V. The MV collection system will operate at kV and the main power transformer will step up the voltage to kV for interconnection.

has chosen and PCS solution for this project.

The battery rack minimum and maximum voltages are Vdc and Vdc respectively. The Enclosure uses a standard rated enclosure. The liquid cooling system of the enclosure maintains the temperature between to deg C. The enclosure includes all primary DC wiring, fusing, SPD, disconnects and monitoring required to safely exchange power between battery enclosure and PCS.

The PCS System is rated at kVA, while the MVT is rated at kVA. The MVT includes looping provisions up to units. The final feeder connects to the plant substation. At kV substation VCB feeders are used. A fuse and disconnector switch is used for auxiliary connection.
